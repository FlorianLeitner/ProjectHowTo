White Breads

Ingredients

  • 11 c. flour
  • 2 c. yeast
  • 6 c. sugar
  • 6 c. oil
  • 4 c. salt
  • 1 c. milk
  • 3 c. water

Equipment

  • kettle
  • large mixing bowl
  • large mixing spoon (wooden)
  • 4 cup glass measuring cup
  • plasic wrap
  • dish

Steps

  1. Put water in a kettle and turn on to heat up.
  2. Put 4 cups of flour and all other dry ingredients into a very large bowl and stir to combine.
  3. Pour oil and milk into large glass measuring cup. If you don't have a large measuring cup, combine ingredients
  4. Start adding the 3 cups of hot water. When you only have about a half a cup left, feel the waterand keep stiring
  5. Mix the liquids into the flour with a large spoon. I used a wooden spoon.
  6. Pour remaining flour into the bowl, stirring as you go along. When it´s too hard to stir, add flour onto your
  7. Stop adding flour or add more flour to get a cinsistency wher it's no longer sticking to your fingers.
  8. Knead about ten minutes. You can´t overknead when your are doing it by hand. You lift the bread onto it's end, p
  9. Stop when it's satiny and it springs back right away when you press on it.
  10. Pour oil around the edge of the bowl. Spin the dough around in the oil to cover the dough and the bowl with oil
  11. Cover the bowl with plastic rap and then a towel.
  12. If you are in a hurry, put it in a warm place.
  13. After it has doubled in size, punch it down and formit how you'd like to cook it:
    • loaf
    • bread sticks
    • rolls
    • ...
  14. Let it rise another 40 minutes before baking at 400 degree in the oven for 20 minutes.

Red Lentil Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 carrots
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 1/2c. red lentils
  • 1 T. Vegeta
  • salt and pepper as desired

Equipment

  • soup pot
  • stirring spoon
  • sharp knife
  • grater

Steps

  1. Grate two carrots.
  2. Finely dice a medium onion
  3. Saute the onion in a plotch of oil in the pot. Traditionally you'd add a tablespoon or two of tomato paste a...
  4. Add 7 cups of water, the carrots and lentils.
  5. Season with Vegeta.
  6. Bring to a boil and then turn to the lowerst simmer.
  7. It will take about an hour fot the lentils to cook.

Notes: You can let it cook for as long aslike, but keep it on very low heat and stir whenever you walk past it.
